What is ReKonnect?

ReKonnect was inspired by the concept of ikigai, which is a Japanese concept that loosely translates to a reason for being.


The Ikigai Model is an intersection of four things as shown in this image. Through these overlapping concepts, you can track down the purpose of your life.


The ideology behind the same is that your Ikigai is the union point of four fundamental components of life:


  • Passion,
  • Mission,
  • Profession, and
  • Vocation

It is an earnest yet a small effort at our end to provide you with an avenue that helps you find your ikigai.

Why ReKonnect?

ReKonnect is not just an employment portal - we focus on your employability. Our goal is to enable the best version of people and their careers - A space for people to equip themselves with all they need, to pursue what their calling truly is.


We help you connect with industry experts and help you become even more attractive through our curated up-skilling programs, hence making you job-ready and getting you closer to what’s needed from a prospective employer


If you are immediately available to join the workforce, you are in the right place
- You could be a fresher looking to make a start
- You could be an experienced professional coming from a break looking to re-start
- You could be looking to build a brand new career in an absolutely new field


Your reasons, your current location don't matter - what matters is your attitude and your determination, and we help you see that


We just need the following details from you during the time of registration:
  • Updated CV
  • Visume (a video introduction < 45 seconds)
  • Minimum three professional references from your last employer(s) (including at least 1 Reporting Manager and HR)
